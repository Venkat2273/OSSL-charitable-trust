Our Mission
To uplift mankind & Society through the pursuit of Education, Social Services & medical facilities at the highest level of excellence.

Om Shri Charitable Trust is non profit organization dedicated to providing professional health care services to the deserving poor.

The trust was formed with an avowed mission of providing modern facilities of Health, Education and Social upliftment in and around the rural areas.

The main object of the trust was to create awareness in the rural people regarding the need of coming out from their age old cultural moorings, customs and superstitions and cultivate a sense of unity in philanthropic activities so as to have a congenial atmosphere to livelihood of self employment.
